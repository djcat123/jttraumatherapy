About JT Trauma Therapy

JT Trauma Therapy is designed for young adults and professionals who want fast access to credible mental health support. No call‑centre scripts, no endless forms – just clear communication and a safe space to process what you’ve been through.

What We Offer

  • 1:1 online trauma‑focused therapy sessions
  • Short‑term stabilisation plans after difficult events
  • Tools for flashbacks, anxiety and sleep issues
  • Referral letters where clinically appropriate

Who It’s For

  • Students balancing stress, study and life pressure
  • Young professionals dealing with burnout or crisis
  • People recovering from sudden or long‑term trauma
  • Anyone who wants support without stigma or judgement

Meet the Team

JT Trauma therapists are qualified, insured professionals with experience in trauma‑focused approaches such as EMDR, CBT and integrative counselling. You’ll be matched with someone based on your goals, schedule and preferences.
